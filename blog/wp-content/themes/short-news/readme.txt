== Description ==
Short News theme is perfect for a news, newspaper, magazine or publishing site. Well documented and easy to use even for WordPress beginners. Share your news, stories, ideas and engage your users with a clean reading experience.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to manually install a WordPress theme? =
1. After downloading the zip file, go to Appearance > Themes and click on the *Install Themes* tab.
2. Click on the *Upload* link.
3. Upload the zip file that you downloaded and click *Install Now*.
4. Click *Activate* to use the theme you just installed.

= How do I Setup my theme? =
Please refer to our theme documentation:
https://www.designlabthemes.com/short-news-documentation/

= How can I get Support? =
Please visit https://wordpress.org/support/theme/short-news/ if you have any questions about the theme.
